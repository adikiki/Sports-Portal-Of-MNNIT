About

Student's Activity Center acts as a lab of HUMAN PERFORMANCE and a hub of enormous extra-curricular and co-curricular activities. SAC has played a key role in grooming the everyday lives of MNNITian's cultivating and nurturing their extra-curricular talents. It seeks to develop qualities of leadership amongst students to foster a corporate life and to advance the general cause of the student body. The various activities of the SAC are coordinated by the student committees, captains, vice captains coordinators etc. Students are exposed to almost all the sports providing an active and vibrant extracurricular life. Since the time of its establishment SAC has striven to motivate the students to be a good leader and a good human being.

Administration

All the activities are run under the administration of the President SAC, faculty co-ordinators, dy.co- ordinators in coordination with the sports officer and a team of student’s committees, captains, vice-captains and various other students’ co-ordinators.
